Frequently Asked Questions

How do the controls work?

The car moves automatically. Use arrow keys to steer left/right, up key for nitro boost (when available), and down key for emergency brake. Space bar triggers handbrake for drifting.

How do I unlock new vehicles?

Complete races and earn cash rewards to unlock up to 45 unique vehicles. Each vehicle has different stats for speed, handling, and durability. Different cars perform better on different terrains.

What are the best strategies to escape police?

Use narrow alleys to break line of sight, drive through tunnels to lose helicopter pursuit, and create roadblocks using civilian vehicles. Change districts to reset pursuit intensity.

How does the scoring system work?

Score points through longer pursuits, near misses with traffic, and drift combos. Daily missions offer premium rewards, and top 100 weekly players earn exclusive cars.

What different environments are available?

Race through city streets, countryside roads, and desert landscapes. Each environment has unique challenges: urban areas have dense traffic, country roads feature sudden obstacles, and deserts require skilled drifting.

How can I improve my performance?

Practice precise steering, learn each vehicle's handling limits, collect power-ups during chases, and choose vehicles suited to specific terrains. Complete daily challenges for bonus rewards.

Is the game free to play?

Yes! Escape Road is completely free to play in your browser. No download or installation required.

How do vehicle upgrades work?

Upgrade engine for better acceleration, enhance tires for improved handling, install nitro for speed boosts, and upgrade armor to survive more hits.

Game not loading or running slowly?

Try refreshing the page, clearing browser cache, or using a different browser. Ensure stable internet connection and close unnecessary browser tabs.

How do weekly tournaments work?

Compete against other players for the highest scores. Top performers earn exclusive rewards and vehicles. New tournaments start every Monday.
